Established 2005

Excellence in Every Culinary Craft

Avora greens was founded upon a singular vision: to create a luxury sanctuary where impeccable service meets world-class gastronomy. Over two decades, we have evolved from a boutique estate venue in Mayfair into a recognized symbol of high-end event catering across the country.

We believe that hospitality is an art form. Every detail, from the polish of the silver champagne flutes to the precise presentation of a pan-seared sea bass, is designed to generate a feeling of refined luxury.

Our Mission

To design custom events and gourmet menus that elevate ordinary celebrations into grand legendary memories through sophisticated styling and Michelin-level kitchen operations.

Our Vision

To remain the premier benchmark of elite event catering and hotel hosting, inspiring our patrons with seasonal dining innovations and unparalleled spatial designs.
